Graphic Designer • Developer • Multi-disciplined Artist

Ahron Jieanne Kent U. Miranda

Ahron has built a reputation for delivering bold, impactful visual solutions tailored to each client's unique vision. Two years as Chief Graphics Designer for his school's publication took him to the National Schools Press Conference stage, where he won as a Layout Artist in Collaborative Desktop Publishing – English. He now brings that same editorial precision to code, building interfaces as carefully composed as a printed page.

Section 06

What this work is in service of.

"In order to reach your full potential, you must create more than you consume."

Ahron is an advocate for student press freedom and for design education that starts young. Through his years in campus journalism, he has mentored incoming layout artists at his school's publication, walking them through grid systems and type hierarchy from scratch.

He believes design and code are the same discipline wearing different tools — both are about making information easy to trust and easy to act on.
